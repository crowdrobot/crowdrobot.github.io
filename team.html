CHATBOT

Crowdbot is a team of students from Germany, Russia, Ukraine and Moldova.
There are 7 of us. We have no directors, each one of us is driven by the idea of improving
the world and self-development.

Everything started in February 2018, with an idea of a tool for NGO's and communities
of all kinds, to create chatbots for various organizational processes, such as task management
and decision-making. While developing this app, we came to a slightly different approach. Instead of better
connecting the members of an organization between themselves, we help NOG's, artists,
creators and change-makers to connect with their supporters and donators as well.

By doing this, we help civic society and creators to find a sustainable way of financing their activities and communicating their
ideas.
